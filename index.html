About

Built to last.
Made to matter.

Wilcrest LLC is a North Carolina-based holding company focused on building strong businesses, investing in the communities around us, and creating value that endures well beyond the balance sheet.

We take a long-term view. Whether we're growing a company from the ground up or partnering with an established team, we bring steady hands, clear thinking, and genuine commitment to every endeavor we undertake.

Our work spans multiple industries, but our approach is always the same: move deliberately, act with integrity, and build things worth being proud of.

  • Build

    We create and grow businesses with strong foundations, from idea through execution and beyond.

  • Invest

    Capital and attention go hand in hand. We invest thoughtfully in people, places, and businesses that align with our values.

  • Grow

    Sustainable growth for our companies, our partners, and the communities where we live and work.
